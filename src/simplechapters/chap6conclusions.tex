\chapter{Conclusions}
\label{ch:conclusions}

This thesis has explored applying SDRs to radar applications.
Radar would be an effective solution to reducing the risks of avalanches
and making the mountains safer to enjoy and explore.
SDRs are versatile and capable devices that can make powerful radios.
Because of this they can often substitute much more complex and expensive systems.
The Lime-mini SDR can function as a radar but its limitations make it unsuitable for avalanche detection.
Results in this thesis have shown that the USRP B210 can function as short-range radar.
With minimal changes they also could be applied to mid-range radar applications
such as radar that could be used for avalanche detection.
